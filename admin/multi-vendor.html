Multivendor

A multivendor marketplace is an online platform where multiple sellers can showcase and sell their products through a single storefront.

Create New Store:

To start selling as a vendor, simply sign up to create your online store.

Fill in all the details, to set up your store.

Once you've signed up, you'll be redirected to the dashboard.

Dashboard

Dashboard Statistics
Total Revenue :

The total earnings from the orders.

Total Refunds :

Number of requests for returns or refunds.

Total Orders :

This shows the number of purchases made by customers.

Total Product :

Number of products available for purchase.

Total Balance :

The amount of Wallet Balance will be showcased here.

Order Status

The relevant details of the orders will be displayed here.

You can use the dropdown menu on the right to filter the status of your order.
